2. Key Components of Digital Education Online Learning Platforms: Websites and applications like Coursera, Khan Academy, and Udemy provide structured courses for different subjects. E-Learning Tools: Interactive simulations, educational apps, and gamified content make learning more engaging. Artificial Intelligence (AI): AI analyzes student performance and customizes learning paths based on individual needs. Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR): These technologies create realistic simulations for subjects like science, medicine, and engineering. Cloud Computing: Allows students and teachers to store and access educational materials anytime, anywhere.
