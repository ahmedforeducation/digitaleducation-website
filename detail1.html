1. Evolution of Digital Education Digital education has evolved significantly over the years. Initially, it began with simple computer-based training programs and CD-ROM courses. With the rise of the internet, online learning platforms such as MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) emerged, offering high-quality education to students worldwide. Today, AI-driven learning, virtual reality (VR), and augmented reality (AR) are enhancing digital education, making it more immersive and personalized.
